Hypertrophy

Hypertrophy, or maximized muscle growth, offers numerous benefits beyond just aesthetics. By increasing muscle size, it enhances overall strength, supports joint stability, and improves physical performance in both everyday activities and athletic pursuits. Additionally, building lean muscle boosts metabolism, helping your body burn more calories at rest, while contributing to better posture and injury prevention.
